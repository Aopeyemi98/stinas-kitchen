Augustina Egbuna, CEO, Stina's Kitchen

Augustina serves as the HEAD of CHEF and CEO of STINA'S KITCHEN with her Chef expertise and entrepreneurial skills. She's a citizen of Nigeria and was born 15th of August. She hail from Anambra State, Idemili North Local Government. She graduated with a degree in BIOCHEMISTRY from OLABISI ONABANJO UNIVERSITY, Ogun State, NIigeria.

Augustina embarked on her cooking business after drawing attention from her mentors and colleagues. Stina's Kitchen offer healthy and consumable products and services to individuals through provision of high quality foods with a good taste of satisfaction. We are Located in Lagos State. Our services includes delivery of bulk and packed meal, small chops and Event Catering.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make healthy and consumable products easily accessible to everyone, cost effective and seamless while maintaining highly, healthy and affordable services.

Our Vission

Our vission is to achieve the highest standard in the Global food Industry while establishing our brand as the preferred brand in Nigeria.
